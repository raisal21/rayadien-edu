#### Inti Materi

- **Tempe** → *Rhizopus oligosporus/oryzae*.

- **Kecap** → *Aspergillus wentii/oryzae*.

- **Tapai & Roti** → *Saccharomyces cerevisiae* (hasilkan CO₂/alkohol).

- **Oncom** → *Neurospora sitophila*; **Keju biru** → *Penicillium roqueforti*.

#### Contoh

- *Penicillium notatum* → antibiotik **penisilin**.

- *Aspergillus niger* → **asam sitrat**; *Trichoderma* → enzim.
