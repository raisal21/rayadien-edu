#### Inti Materi

- **Arus panas** (dari ekuator ke kutub): **Gulf Stream**, **Kuroshio**, **Brazil**, **Agulhas**.
- **Arus dingin** (dari kutub ke ekuator): **Labrador**, **Oyashio**, **Humboldt/Peru**, **Benguela**, **California**.
